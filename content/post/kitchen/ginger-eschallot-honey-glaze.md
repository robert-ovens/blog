---

A glaze for chicken wings or a great dipping sauce.
<!--more-->

ingredients:
  - 1/2 cup honey
  - 1 tbsp sirracha (or more to taste)
  - 1 tbsp ginger paste
  - 2 tbsp very finely chopped eschallots 
  - 1 tsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp mirin or seasoned rice wine
  - 1 tsp dark soy
method:
  - Add it all together. Glaze something.
